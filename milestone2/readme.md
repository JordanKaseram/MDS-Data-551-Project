# Retail Strategy Dashboard 

Welcome to the **Retail Strategy Dashboard**! This project is designed to transform raw retail data into actionable insights for marketing managers and strategy leaders, helping them move from high-level business metrics to specific growth tactics.

---

## 🌟 For Users: Why This Dashboard?

### The Motivation
Retail marketing managers often struggle to balance **sales volume** with **actual profitability**. High-revenue products sometimes carry thin margins, and promotional campaigns can inadvertently erode profits if pricing guardrails aren't established. 

### The Problem We Solve
This dashboard acts as a decision-support tool that bridges the gap between executive performance and granular product strategy. It helps managers:
* **Identify Growth Drivers:** Distinguish between products that drive revenue vs. those that protect the bottom line.
* **Monitor Business Health:** Track sales trends and profit margins across different categories (2011-2014).

### High-Level Features
Currently, the dashboard allows you to:
* **Executive Overview:** Monitor annual sales and profit trends at a glance.
* **Category Deep-Dives:** Compare performance across Technology, Furniture, and Office Supplies to see which groups drive revenue vs. profit.
* **Profitability Analysis:** Use the interactive "Top 10 Products" chart to identify high-impact individual items.
